How to play

1.Select your spaceship

2A. Tablet and Mobile Navigation

Use the navigation buttons to move left and right.

2B. Computer Navigation

Use the left and right arrow keys to move.

3A. Mobile and Tablet Shoot

Tap the circle button to shoot.

3B. Computer Shoot

Press the space bar to shoot.

4.Points

Hit the asteroids to earn points.

5.Lives

You can be hit by asteroids up to three times;
after that, the game ends.

6.Asteroid Speed Calculation

The asteroid speed is calculated based on
the number of Geomagnetic Storms
recorded in the last 30 days.
